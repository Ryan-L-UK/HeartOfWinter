You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. As an action, you can thrust the lance in a direction and fly up to 30 feet in a straight line in that same direction. This movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks. Each creature in the line is buffeted by a powerful gust of wind and must make a DC 15 Strength saving throw.
