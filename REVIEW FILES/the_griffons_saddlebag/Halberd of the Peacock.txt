Halberd of the Peacock
Weapon (halberd), rare (requires attunement)
___

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

This weapon has been polished to a mirror finish and is adorned with five ornate gemstones dangling beneath its blade that glow with arcane energy. This weapon contains five charges, each stored within one of the gems, and regains 1d4+1 expended charges each dawn. On your turn, you can expend one of this weapon's charges to cast disguise self, or two charges to cast alter self, mirror image, or shatter at their lowest levels (spell save DC 14). The hanging gems lose their luminance once their charge has been spent.

In addition, while admiring yourself, you automatically fail any Dexterity saving throws you make until the start of your next turn.
