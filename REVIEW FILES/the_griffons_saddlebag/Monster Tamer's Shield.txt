Monster Tamer's Shield
Armor (shield), rare (requires attunement)
___

This shield punishes certain creatures for their hostility and comes from a culture whose dominion is in direct competition with powerful beasts. While holding this shield, whenever you take damage from a melee attack by a beast or monstrosity, that creature takes 1d4 psychic damage. In addition, you have advantage on Wisdom (Animal Handling) checks.
