Squasher
Weapon (greatclub), uncommon
___

This enormous, magical squash is strangely durable. Druids wielding Squasher are proficient with the weapon, and can use their Wisdom modifier for the attack and damage rolls with it instead of Strength. Squasher functions as a normal magical greatclub if the wielder is not a Druid.
