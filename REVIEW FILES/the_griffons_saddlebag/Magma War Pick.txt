Magma War Pick
Weapon (war pick), rare
___

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you land a hit with this war pick, the impact deals an additional 1d4 fire damage.

This weapon has 3 charges and regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. When you hit a creature that is either made of metal or wearing metal armor, you can expend a charge to inflict an extra 2d8 fire damage to the creature.
