Frost Giant Fork
Weapon (trident), rare (requires attunement)
___

This massive utensil comes from the table of a frost giant. Latent jotun magic flows through this make-shift trident and drives your hunger to nearly aggressive levels. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon, and once per turn when you successfully land a melee weapon attack using the fork against a creature, you can immediately attempt a bite attack against that target using a bonus action. Your jaws and teeth are fortified to have the strength and density of the weapon's original owner, allowing this follow-up bite attack to deal 1d6 piercing damage. You are proficient with the attack and use your Strength modifier when rolling for its attack and damage while you are attuned to the weapon.
