Shadow Drinker
Weapon (dagger), very rare (requires attunement)
___

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls you make with this magic weapon.

When you hit another creature with this dagger with a melee attack, you can absorb the creature's shadow into the dagger for up to 10 minutes. While you have a creature's shadow absorbed, you are considered invisible while in dim light or darkness. If you successfully land an attack from invisibility with this dagger, it harnesses the malevolent shadow to deal an additional 1d6 necrotic damage. This damage increases to 2d6 if the attack was against the shadow-less creature. After you make an attack or cast a spell while you are invisible, the shadow is released and you become visible once more.

You may only have one creature's shadow absorbed at a time and cannot steal a creature's shadow more than once every 24 hours. You can choose to release an absorbed shadow early as a bonus action on your turn.
