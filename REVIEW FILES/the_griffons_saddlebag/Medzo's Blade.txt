Medzo's Blade Weapon (rapier), rare
___

The red and black hilt of this weapon is decorated with four card suits, and its blade has seven hash marks carved into its side. This magic weapon deals 2d6 piercing damage on a hit, instead of 1d8. When you hit with an attack using this rapier and the sum of its damage dice is 7, the next attack roll you make with it before the end of your next turn is made with advantage.
