Moonswaddled Armor
Armor (any light armor), rare
___

This comfortable set of armor grants additional respite when worn at night. You gain a +1 bonus to AC while wearing this armor between sunset and sunrise,  and when you would normally roll one or more hit dice to restore hit points when taking a short rest at night, you instead use the highest number possible for each die.
