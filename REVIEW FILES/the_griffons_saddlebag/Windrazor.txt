Windrazor
Weapon (longsword), legendary (requires attunement)
___

You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. This weapon has the finesse property. The sword has 10 charges and regains 1d6+4 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding the blade, you can expend one of its charges to cast *heroism,* or three charges to cast *wind wall* (save DC 18). Your spellcasting modifier is +5 when casting *heroism* with this weapon.
